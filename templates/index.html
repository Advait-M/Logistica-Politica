What is Logistica Politica?

Politics often becomes an emotionally charged topic. When people gather into groups, certain emotions tend to spread easily within the group, like anger towards another group or fear about the future. But are certain groups more likely to share certain emotions over others? That's what Logistica Politica wants to answer. By analyzing tweets from a user, we can find their political leaning, what emotion they convey in their tweets, and how that compares to other people within their political group.
